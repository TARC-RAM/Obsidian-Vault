## 1) Internet Access Technologies
There are many ways to connect users and organizations to the internet:
- Popular services for home users and small offices include:
	- [[Broadband cable]]
	- [[Digital subscriber line (DSL)]]
	- Wireless [[WAN]]s
	- Mobile services
-  Organizations need faster connections to support IP phones, video conferencing and data center storage.
- Business-Class interconnections are usually provided by service providers and may include:
	- [[Business DSL]]
	- [[Leased lines]]
	- [[Metro Ethernet]]
