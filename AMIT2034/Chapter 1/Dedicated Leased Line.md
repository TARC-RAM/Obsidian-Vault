## Meaning broken down
- **Reserved circuits** → The service provider sets aside a line just for you. No one else uses it.
- **Within the service provider's network** → It’s managed by the ISP or telecom company.
- **Connect distant offices** → Lets two or more locations communicate directly.
- **Private voice and/or data networking** → You can use it for phone calls or internet/data traffic, and it’s **not shared with the public internet**.
